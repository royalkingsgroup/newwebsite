Founder & Chairman

Mr. Sirajudeen Bin Syed Mohamed

Mr. Sirajudeen Bin Syed Mohamed is a visionary entrepreneur and business leader with over 20 years of experience spanning finance facilitation, agro-commodities, wholesale gold bullion (PSPM licensed), information technology, corporate services, hospitality advisory, global trading, and fintech.

He founded Royal Kings Group in 2013 and successfully transformed it into a diversified, technology-enabled enterprise providing integrated business solutions across Asia-Pacific and the Middle East, including key GCC markets. Today, the Group supports businesses through finance facilitation, IT solutions, corporate compliance, hospitality advisory, global trade, and fintech innovation.

With a strong foundation developed within Singapore's highly regulated business environment, Mr. Sirajudeen is recognised for his strategic leadership, governance expertise, and ability to drive sustainable growth across multiple jurisdictions. As Founder & Chairman, he oversees corporate strategy, investments, partnerships, risk management, fintech initiatives, and international expansion.

His leadership philosophy is built on accountability, transparency, and long-term value creation, enabling organisations to scale while maintaining strong governance and operational excellence.

Education & Professional Qualifications

  • MBA, University of Northampton
  • Professional Diploma in Leadership & People Management, Kaplan Singapore
  • Certification in Corporate Secretarial Practice
  • PDPA Operational Compliance Certification, Singapore Management University
  • WSQ Cybersecurity Awareness Certification

Community Leadership & Philanthropy

Mr. Sirajudeen actively supports initiatives focused on education, humanitarian relief, migrant worker welfare, grassroots empowerment, and cultural preservation. He works closely with community organisations, civic groups, and social service partners to strengthen community resilience and promote inclusive development.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, he led large-scale mask and protective equipment distribution efforts through strategic partnerships, supporting vulnerable communities and frontline responders.
